I am a Junior Research Fellow (JRF) in Computer Science at Trinity College, Oxford. I also hold research positions at the University of Oxford (Associate Researcher) and the University of Cambridge (Visiting Researcher). My work focuses on concept-based interpretability, representation learning, and human-AI collaboration. To find out more about my research, please see my research page or visit my Google Scholar profile

Prior to my current position, I did a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Cambridge, where I was lucky to be supervised by Mateja Jamnik and was supported by a Gates Cambridge scholarship.

If you would like to collaborate or chat, feel free to send me an email. I am always happy to connect!
